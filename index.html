ITEMS

For all physical items found in Stranger Things

Kellogg's Eggo

Eggo is a brand of frozen waffles made by the Kellogg Company, and sold in North America. Eleven is obsessed with them.

Spiked Bat

The spiked bat is a weapon created by Jonathan Byers to defeat the Demogorgon, before it was used by Steve Harrington against Demodogs.

Supercoms

Mike Wheeler, Dustin Henderson, Lucas Sinclair and Will Byers each owned a walkie-talkie to maintain contact with each other. These walkie-talkies, also referred to as Supercoms, are reminiscent to the Radio Shack's Realistic TRC-214 brands.
